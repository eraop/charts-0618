## Keyboard Navigation

### Tab Key Navigation

- Using the `Tab` key: Press the `Tab` or `Shift + Tab` key to move switch the focus between the `Series` and the `Legend` in the chart.

### Keyboard Shortcuts

- `Left` or `Right`:

  - Press the `Left` or `Right` key to move switch between different Legends.
  - Press the `Left` or `Right` key to move forward or backward on the chart Series

- `Up` or `Down`: Press the `Up` or `Down` key to move switch between different `Series`.
